1 About

I work as a Future Faculty Teaching Fellow in the Department of Philosophy, Religion, and Classics at Butler University. I am a PhD candidate in philosophy at Indiana University Bloomington, with a PhD minor in the history and philosophy of science and medicine. I received an MA in philosophy, a BA in philosophy, and a BA in economics from Peking University.

Outside of philosophy, I like avant-garde cinema, experimental arts, rock music (classic, punk, indie), watching sports, and enjoying good beer. Recently, I enjoy exploring different writing tools that could facilitate my academic life, e.g. Emacs, Atom.
