1 About

I work as a Future Faculty Teaching Fellow at Butler University. I am a PhD candidate in philosophy at Indiana University Bloomington, with a PhD minor in the history and philosophy of science and medicine. I received an MA in philosophy, a BA in philosophy, and a BA in economics from Peking University.

Areas of Specialization: Metaphysics, Chinese Philosophy

Areas of Competence: Ethics, Applied Ethics, Philosophy of Science, Philosophy of Language, Philosophy of Religion

Outside of philosophy, I like avant-garde cinema, rock music (classic, punk, indie), watching sports, and enjoying good beer.
